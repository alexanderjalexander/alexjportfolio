ABOUT

Ever since middle school and high school, I've always been extremely intrigued in how film and online content can be used to not only entertain, but to transport us to new worlds and dimensions, often creating home movies with a camcorder and iMovie for fun, creating my own stories based off of things I liked. As I grew up, I started to try my hands with online content, making gaming videos, upgrading to a new non-linear editor, and learning more intricate editing techniques to create something entirely new within my own niche.

As of now, I edit humorous montages of the hit game Apex Legends on YouTube, and have performed commission-based work for various content creators on YouTube.

I have experience editing with MAGIX VEGAS Pro, Adobe Premiere Pro, and Adobe After Effects.
