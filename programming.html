I started off my programming experience in late middle school and early high school, learning C# through the Unity Game Engine, and developing games as part of a team through NJIT's RWC program. Afterwards, I learned Java in AP Computer Science A and decided to enroll in Stevens Institute of Technology's Computer Science program.

My relevant coursework includes: Computer Architecture and Organization, Systems Programming, Principles of Programming Languages, Discrete Structures, Data Structures, Algorithms, Series and Vectors, Multivariable Calculus, Probability and Statistics, and Linear Algebra.

I have experience programming in Java, OCaml, C++, C, C#, Scheme, Python, Bash, Assembly (ARMv8), HTML, and CSS.
