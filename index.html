About Alex

Hello there! My name's Alex; I'm a 2/4 Computer Science student at Stevens Institute of Technology and I am based in NJ. I am a creative and open-minded person committed to exploring the many ways I can express myself creatively or create exciting new projects through various forms of technology and media.

For many years, I've focused a lot of my passion into programming and various art forms surrounding design, multimedia, and technology. In addition to my studies as a Computer Science major, I enjoy programming my own projects. In the past(middle school and high school), I have avidly worked with friends and teams to code and develop games as part of NJIT's RWC program. I carry that same passion into my current projects today, such as a logical proposition interpreter based entirely in Java.

Some of my range of artistic work and explorations typically involve video editing, animation, or some sort of graphic design. For many years, I have developed a love for video editing, having edited personal montages for my own YouTube channel as well as commissions for other notable content creators. I am always open to trying out any new form of art or piece of technology to express my imagination(e.g. coding this website from the ground up!).

As each day passes, one of the questions I always ask myself is "How can I make this better?" or "Is there a way I can represent this in a new way?" To me, boundaries in creative expression and technology should always be pushed to new directions and forms. Keeping oneself limited to what we only think is possible will only hinder us in the end, thus the reason for my extensive involvement in programming, my hobbies, and other various projects.

I hope that as you scroll through each part of this web page, you get a sense of who I am as an individual and how I blend my own creative inspirations into the things that I create!
